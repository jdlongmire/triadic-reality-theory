# Gravity: The Linearity Test (GR vs Modified Gravity)

> **Tier:** 3-prediction (Popperian severe test). **Lakatos role:** the discriminating *observational consequence* of the gravity reduction. The reduction *work* lives in the belt at [`../2-theory/03-gravity/`](../2-theory/03-gravity/); the *falsifiable claim* lives here. **Confidence:** LOW; UNCERTAIN pending formalization.

## The fork, as a prediction

The gravity reduction chain ([linearity-crux](../2-theory/03-gravity/linearity-crux.md), [relational-graph-test](../2-theory/03-gravity/relational-graph-test.md)) reduced "does TRT give GR?" to one combinatorial question about the co-admissibility measure: **is it linear or quadratic in the holonomy deficit?**

- **Linear** (raw obstruction) → exact General Relativity, via Regge + Lovelock, with **no higher-curvature corrections from this mechanism**.
- **Quadratic** (best-fit residual / information distance) → Bianconi-class modified gravity, with curvature-squared corrections.

This is governed by the [keystone](../2-theory/00-foundational/actualization-resolution.md): whether actualizing action *A* registers obstruction or best-fit projects.

## The discriminating content (`falsifies`)

Linear and quadratic measures **differ observationally at high curvature** — near black holes and in the early universe.

- If nature shows **no** higher-curvature corrections of the predicted form, the linear/GR branch is supported and the quadratic branch is disfavored.
- If nature shows higher-curvature corrections matching the Bianconi-class form, the quadratic branch is supported and the strict-GR branch is falsified.

This is a genuine discriminating prediction (the program's Tier 4.2 bar), and it is the same fork seen from the data side.

## The gravity co-admissibility conjecture (§13.4)

> A formal criterion of global co-admissibility will recover the constraint structure of general relativity — the divergence-free Einstein tensor, the field-equation form, and the black-hole area law including its coefficient — from a statement of global *L₃*-consistency over relational structure; **or** connect to an information-geometric entropic action in an appropriate limit.

**Fails if** the formalized criterion is compatible with multiple non-equivalent gravitational structures equally well, cannot distinguish Einstein gravity from the broader class of generally covariant theories, or cannot recover the known area law in the appropriate black-hole limit.

## Status

Either resolution of the fork — linear or quadratic — is a progressive result when recorded.
