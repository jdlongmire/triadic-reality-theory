# The Effectiveness of Mathematics (Retrodiction)

> **Tier:** 3-prediction. **Lakatos role:** a **retrodiction**, not a forward prediction — a standing puzzle that competing ontologies treat as brute, which TRT turns into a structural expectation. **Confidence:** MEDIUM (paper §9.5), with a weaker companion retrodiction on the discipline of foundational mathematics (§3.4.2, MEDIUM).

## The claim

Wigner (1960) framed the effectiveness of mathematics in physics as *unreasonable* — a fit between abstract structure and the physical world that competing ontologies treat as unexplained.

TRT removes the puzzle by predicting the fit. If actualized reality is necessarily logically constrained and informationally structured (the synthetic necessity of paper §9.3), then mathematics is not an external instrument that happens to apply — **mathematics is what logical constraint operating on informational potential generates.** It is the structure of the admissible. Two of TRT's three necessary constituents (*L₃* and *I∞*) are precisely the constituents from which mathematics arises, so actualized reality necessarily takes a form mathematics describes.

## Why it counts as evidence (and how much)

Its weight lies in **retrodiction**: a standing puzzle that competing ontologies treat as brute is, on TRT, a structural expectation. A framework that turns an acknowledged anomaly into a prediction earns support proportionate to how widely the anomaly is recognized and how poorly it is otherwise explained.

This is offered as a **consequence, not an independent argument for the triad** — it does not, by itself, discriminate TRT from rivals that also predict the fit. Its progressive weight is real but bounded, which is why it is filed as a retrodiction and the discriminating burden remains with the [co-admissibility](co-admissibility-conjecture.md) and [gravity](gravity-linearity-test.md) conjectures.

## Companion (lower confidence)

The discipline of foundations (§3.4.2): if consistent mathematics is *I∞* filtered by *L₃*, the historical restriction of naive comprehension (Russell's type theory, ZF's axiomatic restriction) is what one would expect — the discovery of the gate between representable and admissible. MEDIUM; admits competing intuitionist/formalist readings, so offered as suggestive, not established.
