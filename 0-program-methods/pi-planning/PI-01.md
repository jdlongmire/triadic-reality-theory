# PI-1 Plan

> **Program Increment 1** for the TRT research programme. Planning artifact under [github-workflow.md](../github-workflow.md). The PI is the temporal box (a GitHub **milestone**); tiers are **labels**; the iteration unit is one *problemshift attempt with a stated success/failure condition*.

## Horizon & cadence

**Problemshift-counted, soft calendar.** Five iterations; each is one bounded ops deliverable or one problemshift attempt. Nominal ~2 weeks/iteration (~10 weeks), but an iteration closes on **resolution**, not the clock — research does not commit to a discovery, only to running the attempt.

## Roles

- **Execution (thinx-Claude + spawned agents):** reference verification, LRT vendoring + build, the formalization attempts (math drafts + finite-graph computation), PDF build, draft deposits, traceability updates.
- **Approval gates (JD):** Zenodo publish approval; the *progressive-vs-degenerating* call on any claimed formalization (is it a derivation or re-description?); the appraisal verdict. These are JD's by methodology and by `morals.md` (publish = soft-stop).

## PI Objectives

Value = epistemic value (1–10): does it advance the programme toward *doing physics*?

| # | Objective | Type | Value | Done = |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **O1** | First citable artifact: position paper → Zenodo DOI | Committed | 7 | DOI minted, RECORD.md + CITATION.cff updated |
| **O2** | LRT formal core imported & green | Committed | 6 | `formalization/lrt/` builds green in CI; `Core` imports the L₃ backbone |
| **O3** | Attempt co-admissibility formalization (Task 0.1) | **Stretch** | 10 | **Resolved**: a measure-free criterion, *or* a recorded proof it needs a free measure |
| **O4** | Decide the linearity crux on a finite graph (Task 3.1) | **Stretch** | 9 | **Resolved**: linear (→GR) or quadratic (→modified), with logical motivation |

**Commitment semantics:** O1, O2 are *committed* — they will land (mechanical/bounded). O3, O4 are *stretch* — the commitment is to **run the attempt to a recorded resolution**, not to a positive result. A recorded refutation closes the objective and is a progressive outcome.

## Iteration plan

```
Iter 1   O1a verify references (issue #1)         ║  O2a vendor LRT core, attempt lake build green
Iter 2   O1b cut v1.0 public copy, build PDF,     ║  O2b wire Core→LRT import, CI green
            draft deposit                          ║  O3· kickoff: finite-graph co-admissibility setup
Iter 3   O1c PUBLISH on JD approval → DOI,         ║  O3· main attempt: define criterion, measure-free?
            RECORD + CITATION
Iter 4   O3· resolve (criterion exists / needs free measure)  →  O4· linearity computation if O3 yields a criterion
Iter 5   O4· resolve (linear vs quadratic)  →  appraisal update  →  PI retro + PI-2 seeding
```

O1 and O2 run in parallel (independent). The DOI lands ~Iter 3. O3 spans Iter 2–4 and **gates** O4.

## Dependencies & critical path

```
O1  ──(independent)──► DOI
O2  ──(independent)──► L₃ backbone available
O3 (Task 0.1) ──gates──► O4 (Task 3.1) ──gates──► the entire discriminating programme
```

**Critical path runs through O3.** Everything that makes TRT *physics rather than ontology* sits downstream of the co-admissibility formalization.

## Risks (ROAM)

| Risk | Status | Handling |
|---|---|---|
| Co-admissibility may not formalize without a free measure | **Accepted** | A recorded negative resolves O3 and is progressive; it is the make-or-break the ROADMAP names. |
| LRT vendored core may not build green under v4.28 | **Mitigated** | Toolchains already matched; sandbox the build before wiring `Core`. |
| A carried-forward citation may not hold up | **Owned** (JD) | Would force a paper edit before v1.0 / before deposit. |
| Stretch work crowds out committed spine | **Mitigated** | O1/O2 are front-loaded and parallel; O3/O4 do not compete for the same effort type. |

## Confidence

- **Committed (O1, O2): HIGH** — bounded, mechanical, no discovery risk.
- **Stretch (O3, O4): HIGH confidence in *running the attempt to resolution*; no confidence vote on the *outcome*** — by design, you do not vote confidence on a discovery.

## Definition of Done (PI-1)

DOI minted and recorded (O1); LRT core green in CI (O2); O3 resolved either way and logged in [appraisal.md](../../3-prediction/appraisal.md); O4 resolved if O3 produced a criterion; appraisal verdict refreshed; PI retro captured and PI-2 seeded.
