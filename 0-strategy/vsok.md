# VSOK — Vision · Strategy · Objectives · Key Results

The strategic frame for the programme. VSOK is the Tier-0 structured artifact (Ologos VWM process: VSOK / WBS / MBSE); here it sits *above* the falsifiability ladder ([0-strategy](.) governs tiers 1–3, it is not on the ladder). The Objectives and Key Results are the [ROADMAP](ROADMAP.md) tiers recast as OKRs.

---

## Vision

A candidate foundational ontology beneath physics and metaphysics: **physical reality is constrained information in action.** Three co-requiring primitives — finite logical constraint *L₃*, infinite informational potential *I∞*, actualizing action *A* — converge as actualized reality *χ ≡ A(I∞ | L₃)*. The vision is to move this from a coherent position to a formalized theory that recovers known physics and issues a discriminating prediction.

## Strategy

**Formalization is the bottleneck, not conceptual elaboration.** Three independent reviews converged: additional philosophical exposition adds weight without rigor. The strategy is therefore single: convert named conjectures into either theorems/computations or recorded failures, in dependency order, maintaining honest confidence labels and stated failure conditions throughout. The credibility of the programme *is* that discipline.

Structurally: a [Lakatosian programme](METHODOLOGY.md) with a transcendentally-warranted hard core, a protective belt of adjustable formal machinery, and this document's OKRs as the positive heuristic. The [LRT formal core](../formalization/lrt/) is imported as the verified formalization of the *L₃* constituent.

## Objectives & Key Results

OKR semantics here are **epistemic**: a Key Result is achieved when a conjecture is *resolved* — a confirmation **or** a recorded refutation both count. Only an undecidable or quietly-abandoned conjecture is a miss.

### O1 — Make co-admissibility a definite, measure-free criterion *(the gating objective)*
- **KR1.1** A criterion `C(constraints) → admissible set`, well-defined, computable, no free entanglement measure. *(Confidence a formalization exists: UNCERTAIN.)*
- **KR1.2** A covariant measure of actualization rate/density. *(LOW.)*
- **KR1.3** Characterize *A*'s resolution operation (the [keystone](../2-theory/00-foundational/actualization-resolution.md)): obstruction-registering or best-fit projective. *(MEDIUM that this is the shared keystone.)*

### O2 — Produce the first discriminating result *(depends on O1)*
- **KR2.1** Compute co-admissibility for the incompatible-channel cavity case; compare against the analytic critical coupling. *(Gated by KR1.1.)*

### O3 — Give the theory variational + measurement structure *(depends on O1)*
- **KR3.1** A definite Lagrangian density over *χ* yielding equations of motion. *(LOW.)*
- **KR3.2** Derive (not assume) the Born measure. *(LOW.)*

### O4 — Decide the gravity fork *(depends on O1, keystone)*
- **KR4.1** Resolve the linearity crux on a finite relational graph: linear (→ GR) or quadratic (→ modified gravity). *(MEDIUM that GR is recoverable conditional on linearity; UNCERTAIN that linearity holds.)*

### O5 — Recover known physics and issue a novel prediction *(depends on all above)*
- **KR5.1** Recover Hilbert space, probability, gauge structure, relativistic spacetime, conservation laws in appropriate limits.
- **KR5.2** A novel discriminating prediction that survives contact with data — the bar for *progressive* status. *(See [appraisal](../3-prediction/appraisal.md): currently unappraised.)*
