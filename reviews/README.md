# Review Archive

Critical reviews of TRT are archived here with responses. A review that forces a confidence label downward, exposes a circularity, or compels a claim to be retracted is a **successful** contribution and is recorded as such — it is a progressive problemshift (see [METHODOLOGY](../0-program-methods/METHODOLOGY.md)).

**Convention:** one subdirectory per review, named `YYYY-MM-source/`, containing the review (`review.md`) and the response (`response.md`). Responses state what was accepted, what was contested, and what changed in the paper or programme as a result.
